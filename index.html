PROBLEM FINDING

Through interview and research, we found that nausea and vomiting are the most common discomforts associated with pregancy.

75% pregnant women encountered vomiting and nausea.

SOLUTION FINDING

We decided to design a mobile app for pregnant women to explore personal NVP pattern and manage daily activities, along with an acupressure wrist band to relief and record vomiting and nausea.

INITIAL DESIGN

Rcording

Moms can record NVP through the accupressure wristband or their mobiles.

Exploring

DOT-IT can interpret NVP data into visualized information for mothers to explore their NVP trend and pattern.

Scheduling

Through the shcedule builder, moms can arrange their plans based on their existing NVP records, aiming to better manage daily activities.

PROTOTYPING

Low-Fidelity Prototype

The low-fidelity prototype focuses on gathering user feedback on what other functions might be useful in the future.

Through the user tests, we decided to add the function of sending NVP notifications to the pregnant women's partners.

High-Fidelity Prototype

The high-fidelity prototype focuses on gathering data of the feasibility of our application as well as the parteners' notification functions' feedback.
